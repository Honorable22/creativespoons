Our Services

Concept. Design. Solution.


We are committed to providing you with the best solutions for your website. Whether you need a newly built website or just maintenance for an existing website. We've got you covered. We offer the following creative services to our clients:

Branding

Modern brand identity establishes the appeal and attraction of your business to the target community. It is important because it essentially dictates how you want your business to be perceived by the public. Some of the main components for branding include name, logo design, and color scheme.

UX/UI Design

User Experience design is the process of conceptualizing, planning, and building the layout of web pages based on brand identity, content strategy, visual content, and use of interactive features. This focuses on having a deep understanding of how users interact with your website and improving this expereince for all new and existing users.

Website Development

Website development is the actual process of writing markup code. This is where the web design comes to life online with an unique domain name and content management system.

Website Maintenance

In order to attract and retain customers, maintain search engine rankings, and provide information about your products and services to the public, your company needs website maintenance. This will allow your content to stay updated, monitors your visitors, and help drive traffic to boost your business goals.

Frequently asked Questions

Your questions. Our answers.


How long does it take to build a website?

Once we have all the written and visual content, it should take approximately 3-6 weeks for us to design and build a typical, small scale website of 5-7 pages. The most time consuming phase of any website project is collecting and writing the content.

Are your websites mobile friendly?

Yes. Every website we design and develope will be compatible with multiple devices and screen sizes.

What are your payment terms?

Generally we require three terms of payment. The first payment is a 50% upfront deposit. The second payment is 25% due at design approval, and the final payment of 25% is due at the launch of your website.

Do you use integrated website builders such as square space, wordpress, or wix?

No. We always hand code our own websites based on your ideas and unique customizations giving you the flexibility to do what you want with your website. With this your website will use the latest tecnologies and best practices without paying an extra monthly service fee. The website will be 100% yours at completion.
